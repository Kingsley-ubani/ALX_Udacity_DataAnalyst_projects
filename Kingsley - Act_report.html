Insights generated from WeRateDogs project¶

This report describes the insights and visualization gotten from the WeRateDogs Project.

Insight 1 - Top 10 most tweeted dog_breed¶

The visuals below shows the top 10 most engaged Dog Breed by tweet.

From the analysis on the WeRateDogs cleaned data shared in the wrangle_act file, the total number of tweets gotten was 2075 tweets. However, for the sake of validation,it is safe to say golden_retriever is the most tweeted dog-breed (having 173 tweets). This is because the top of the chart is an 'unknown' breed which can't be most likely assigned to any breed on the list. The top 5 most tweeted dog-breed are Golden_retriever,Labrador_retriever,Pembroke, Chihuahua and Pug.

Insight 2 - Dog_breed with the most rating¶

The visuals below describes the top 10 Dog-breed with the most rating

From the graph, it can be clearly seen that the top 5 dog-breed with the most rating are Golden_retriever,Labrador_retriever,Pembroke,Chihuahua and Pug. Golden retriever retains it's position as the dog breed with most rating.

This also didn't come as a surprise because the same list is the most tweeted. From findings,it is worthy of note to conclude that these breeds are friendly and make great family pets.

Insight 2 - Dog_name with most number of twitter likes¶

The visual below demonstraes the dog_name with the most number of twitter likes.

The top 5 dog names with the most number of likes are None, Charlie, Lucy, Oliver, Cooper.

None being selected could be as a result of people not hitting the like button on any of the dog_names.
